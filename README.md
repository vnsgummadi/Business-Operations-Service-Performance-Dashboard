## 🗂️ Dataset
The dashboard is built using a public technician and service performance dataset, adapted to simulate a real-world service operations environment.

### 📌 Business Scenario
The dataset represents a company providing household and field services, where technicians perform tasks such as:
- Installation  
- Maintenance  
- Repairs  
- Customer service requests  

Technicians operate across multiple business units, and the dataset tracks their performance over time.

### 📊 Key Data Fields
- Date / Week Beginning  
- Business Unit  
- Technician  
- Jobs Completed  
- Gross Profit  
- Close Rate  
- Booking Rate  
- Sales Rate  
- Performance Notes  

This structure allows for realistic analysis of productivity, efficiency, and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Objectives
The dashboard is designed to answer critical business questions such as:
- How is overall performance trending over time?  
- Which business units contribute the most to revenue and output?  
- Which technicians are driving the highest profit?  
- How many jobs are being completed across the organization?  
- Are conversion metrics (close rate, booking rate) healthy?  
- What operational areas require deeper attention?  

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Gross Profit**  
  Total profit generated within the selected time period  

- **Total Jobs Completed**  
  Total number of service jobs completed  

- **Profit per Job**  
  Average profit generated per completed job  

- **Average Close Rate**  
  Measures how effectively opportunities are converted into completed jobs  

- **Average Booking Rate**  
  Indicates consistency in booking service work  

- **Active Technicians**  
  Number of technicians contributing within the selected view

## 📊 Dashboard Visuals
